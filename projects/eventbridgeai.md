## Description

EventBridge AI is a web-based platform that centralizes community-led events across a city onto a single, intelligent system. Communities such as IEEE, GDSC, and ACM can register, manage their profiles, and handle the full event lifecycle — from creation and publishing to attendance tracking and feedback collection. Users can discover, register for, and engage with events through an AI-powered feed tailored to their interests.

The platform features semantic search powered by vector embeddings, a personalized event recommendation engine, inter-community collaboration tools, and a real-time notification system. An admin panel provides full governance and moderation over communities, users, and events.
