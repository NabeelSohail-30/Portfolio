## Description

NeoHouse is an internal real estate property management platform designed to give teams full operational control over their property portfolio in one place. It covers the complete lifecycle of a property — from ownership records and valuation tracking to tenant rentals, payment schedules, running fees, and document management.

The platform supports management firm and agent assignments per property, tracks repair and maintenance expenses, and includes a full suite of configurable lookup tables (property types, agreement types, rent periods, payment methods, and more). Built on a traditional server-rendered MVC architecture with selective AJAX interactions, NeoHouse prioritizes reliability and straightforward workflows over complexity — making it fast to use and easy to maintain.
